What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, a company founded by former OpenAI researchers. Its primary focus is on safety and reliability.

Claude is particularly strong at understanding long documents, writing nuanced content and following complex instructions without losing context.

FAQ

Is Claude free?
Yes, Claude has a generous free plan. The Pro plan at $20/month unlocks Claude Opus and much higher usage limits.
Claude vs ChatGPT — which is better?
Claude is generally better for long documents and nuanced writing. ChatGPT has a richer ecosystem with plugins and image generation.
Does Claude have memory?
Claude can use Projects to maintain context across sessions. The free version has no persistent memory.
