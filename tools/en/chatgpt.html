What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI assistant based on OpenAI's GPT models. Launched in November 2022, it reached 100 million users in just 2 months — an absolute record in web history.

Unlike a search engine, ChatGPT generates original and contextual responses. It understands language nuances, maintains conversation context and accomplishes complex tasks from creative writing to data analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChatGPT really free?
Yes, the Free plan is genuinely free with no credit card required. It gives access to GPT-4o mini with limits on GPT-4o at peak hours.
What is the difference between ChatGPT and GPT-4?
GPT-4 is the AI model (the 'brain'), ChatGPT is the interface to access it. ChatGPT Plus uses GPT-4o, the most advanced version.
Can ChatGPT access the internet?
Yes, with ChatGPT Plus. The free version has knowledge limited to its training date. The Plus plan browses the web in real time.
Is my data private with ChatGPT?
With the Free plan, OpenAI may use your conversations to improve its models. Team and Pro plans offer full privacy.
