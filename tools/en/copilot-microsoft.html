What is Microsoft Copilot?

Microsoft Copilot is the artificial intelligence assistant integrated across the entire Microsoft 365 ecosystem, powered by OpenAI's GPT-4 and GPT-4 Turbo models. It assists users directly in Word to write and improve documents, in Excel to analyze complex data and generate formulas, in PowerPoint to create complete presentations from a simple text prompt, in Teams to summarize meetings in real time, and in Outlook to intelligently draft and sort emails. Copilot can also conduct web searches via Bing and access files shared within an organization to contextualize its responses. Available through a separate Microsoft 365 Copilot subscription, it represents one of the most significant integrations of generative artificial intelligence into a professional environment to date. Microsoft has also embedded Copilot directly into Windows 11, making it accessible from any application within the operating system. It is fundamentally transforming the way companies and knowledge workers organize their daily productivity, significantly reducing the time spent on repetitive tasks and enabling teams to focus on higher-value strategic activities.

FAQ

Is Copilot free?
Yes, basic Copilot at copilot.microsoft.com is free. Office app integration requires Microsoft 365 Copilot at $30/user/month.
Copilot vs ChatGPT for Excel?
Copilot Excel is superior because it accesses your data directly without copy-pasting. Ideal for complex formulas and analyses.
Does Copilot use my company data?
Copilot uses your M365 data to personalize responses but Microsoft guarantees enterprise privacy.
