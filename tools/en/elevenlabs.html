What is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is the most advanced and recognized voice synthesis and voice cloning platform powered by artificial intelligence currently on the global market. Founded in 2022 in New York by Mati Staniszewski, a former Palantir employee, and Piotr Dąbkowski, the startup quickly reached a valuation exceeding one billion dollars, joining the AI unicorn club. Its voice synthesis technology is unanimously recognized as the most natural, expressive, and realistic available, capable of generating human voices that deceive even trained ears. ElevenLabs offers extensive multilingual coverage spanning more than 29 languages with authentic regional accents and varied emotional tones. Its voice cloning feature allows faithfully recreating anyone's voice from an audio sample of just a few seconds, opening revolutionary possibilities for content localization and voice preservation. The platform is massively used for audiobook production, film and series dubbing, podcast narration, creating voice characters for video games, AI voice assistants, and educational content narration. Content creators, publishers, production studios, and voice application developers adopt ElevenLabs to produce broadcast-quality professional audio content at scale.
