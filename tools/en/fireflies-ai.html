What is Fireflies.ai?

Fireflies.ai is an intelligent professional meeting assistant that uses artificial intelligence to automatically record, accurately transcribe, intelligently summarize, and thoroughly analyze all your business conversations. The platform operates by joining your Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, or Webex meetings as a discreet silent participant called Fred, capturing every exchange without disrupting the natural flow of discussion. After each completed meeting, Fireflies automatically generates a structured and detailed summary, clearly identifies strategic decisions made, lists tasks assigned to each participant with designated owners, and organizes topics discussed by themes to facilitate review. Its advanced semantic search function allows finding any precise moment from a past meeting through a simple natural language query. Fireflies also integrates with many professional tools such as Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Notion, and Asana to automate updates to CRMs and project management tools after each sales call or client meeting. It is an absolutely indispensable tool for sales teams, account managers, startups, and any organization wishing to maximize the productivity of its meetings.
