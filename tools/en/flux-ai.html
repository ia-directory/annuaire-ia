What is Flux AI?

Flux AI is a family of artificial intelligence image generation models developed by Black Forest Labs, a company founded in 2024 by Robin Rombach and other founding members of the original Stable Diffusion team at Stability AI. The Flux family includes several versions with different performance profiles: Flux Schnell, optimized for generation speed, Flux Dev, intended for developers wishing to experiment, and Flux Pro, the premium model offering the best available visual quality. Flux models are praised by the global creator community for their truly exceptional photorealistic quality, superior anatomical consistency in human body rendering, remarkable ability to faithfully follow complex and detailed prompts, and the precision of their rendering of natural and artificial light. Flux Pro ranks among the most capable AI models available in terms of raw image quality. Available via API on platforms such as Replicate, Fal.ai, or directly from the Black Forest Labs interface, Flux quickly won over the professional AI image creation community upon its launch.
