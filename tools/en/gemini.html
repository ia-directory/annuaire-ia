What is Gemini?

Gemini is the multimodal artificial intelligence assistant developed by Google DeepMind, Google's AI research division. Officially launched in December 2023 as the replacement for Bard, Gemini is capable of understanding and generating text, images, audio, video, and programming code. It is available in several versions tailored to different use cases: Gemini Nano for mobile devices, Gemini Pro for general consumer applications, and Gemini Ultra for the most complex tasks requiring maximum computing power. Gemini is directly integrated into Google's flagship products such as Gmail, Google Docs, Google Search, and Google Assistant, radically transforming everyday user experiences. Its ability to simultaneously process multiple types of data distinguishes it from traditional text-only models. It also benefits from real-time access to information through Google Search, making it especially useful for queries requiring up-to-date data. Gemini represents Google's strategic response to the rise of ChatGPT and positions the company at the heart of the generative artificial intelligence revolution.

FAQ

Is Gemini free?
Yes, Gemini Pro is completely free with a Google account. The Advanced plan at $21.99/month gives access to Gemini Ultra, the most powerful model.
Gemini vs ChatGPT: which to choose?
Gemini is better if you already use Google Workspace. ChatGPT has a richer ecosystem with GPTs. For recent facts, Gemini is more reliable.
Can Gemini read my Gmail?
Yes, with your permission. Gemini Advanced can summarize, draft and analyze your emails directly in Gmail.
