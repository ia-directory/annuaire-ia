What is Leonardo AI?

Leonardo AI is an artificial intelligence image generation platform founded in 2022 and specifically designed to meet the needs of video game creators, digital art designers, professional illustrators, and art directors. Where other image generation tools target a general audience, Leonardo focuses on producing coherent, high-quality visual assets suited to game and animation production pipelines. The platform offers models specially trained on game visual styles to generate characters, environments, textures, user interfaces, and objects with remarkable artistic consistency. Its fine-tuning feature allows each user to train their own custom models from provided visual examples, guaranteeing a consistent visual identity across all assets of a project. Leonardo also integrates upscaling tools, advanced editing, and variation generation. The platform is particularly popular among independent game studios, freelance concept artists, and creative teams seeking to significantly accelerate their visual production pipeline while maintaining a high level of artistic quality and stylistic consistency throughout every asset of their project.
