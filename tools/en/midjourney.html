What is Midjourney?

Midjourney is an AI image generation tool accessible via Discord. It transforms text descriptions into high-quality artistic images.

Known for its distinctive aesthetic quality, Midjourney produces results that often surpass other generators in artistic coherence and visual detail.

Currently yes — Midjourney operates through Discord. A web interface is in development.
