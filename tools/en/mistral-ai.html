What is Mistral AI?

Mistral AI is a French startup founded in May 2023 by former researchers from Google DeepMind and Meta AI, including Arthur Mensch, Guillaume Lample, and Timothée Lacroix. Rapidly elevated to European unicorn status, it develops open and extremely high-performing language models. Its flagship models, such as Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B, and Mistral Large, are universally recognized for their remarkable efficiency despite a compact size, making local deployment possible on low-powered servers or even personal computers. Mistral AI champions a European and sovereign vision of artificial intelligence, founded on transparency, open source principles, and independence from American tech giants. Its models are widely adopted by companies wishing to integrate AI internally without relying on foreign providers. La Plateforme offers a competitive commercial API allowing developers to easily access these models to build high-performing and cost-effective AI applications, cementing Mistral's position as the credible European alternative to OpenAI and Google in the generative AI landscape.

FAQ

Is Mistral truly open source?
Yes, Mistral 7B and Mixtral models are open source. Premium models (Large) are proprietary but accessible via API.
Mistral vs ChatGPT in French?
Mistral excels in French thanks to its Parisian team. French linguistic nuances are better understood.
Can I host Mistral locally?
Yes, open source models can be deployed on your own servers via Ollama or HuggingFace.
