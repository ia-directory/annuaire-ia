What is Pi AI?

Pi is a conversational assistant developed by Inflection AI, a startup founded by Mustafa Suleyman, co-founder of DeepMind, and Reid Hoffman, co-founder of LinkedIn. Unlike AI assistants primarily focused on productivity and technical tasks, Pi is designed to offer warm, deeply personal, and genuinely human interactions. Its name literally means Personal Intelligence, reflecting its vocation as a companion AI rather than a tool AI. Pi stands out for its empathetic, patient, and caring tone, particularly well-suited to conversations about emotions, personal decisions, life goals, and mental well-being. It learns the preferences and habits of each user over the course of exchanges to progressively adapt its communication style and recommendations. Pi is accessible via web, iOS, and Android, as well as through SMS, WhatsApp, and Instagram. It is one of the rare AIs that deliberately prioritizes authentic human connection and emotional engagement over raw technical performance alone, making it a truly unique and differentiated offering in the global AI ecosystem.

FAQ

Is Pi really free?
Yes, Pi is completely free with no message limits and no subscription. Inflection AI monetizes through B2B partnerships.
Can Pi replace a therapist?
No. Pi is a support and reflection tool, not a healthcare professional. For serious issues, consult a therapist.
Does Pi speak other languages?
Yes, Pi speaks French, Spanish and many other languages fluently.
