What is Pika Labs?

Pika Labs is an American AI video generation and editing startup founded in 2023 by Demi Guo and Chenlin Meng, two former Stanford PhD students, after raising tens of millions of dollars in venture capital from top-tier investors. Pika's intuitive web platform allows creating original video clips from a simple text description, animating still images by infusing them with movement, modifying the visual style of existing videos, and applying spectacular creative special effects in a minimum of clicks. Its signature feature Pikaffects allows adding dramatic and viral effects such as levitation, explosion, liquefaction, or the transformation of one object into another. Pika 1.5 and its subsequent versions have considerably improved movement fluidity, character consistency over time, and overall video rendering quality. The platform stands out for its remarkable accessibility, making AI video creation possible for users with no technical skills in video editing or animation. Pika is particularly popular and adopted by content creators active on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts for quickly producing eye-catching, original, and viral visuals.
