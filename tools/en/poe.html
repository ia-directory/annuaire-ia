What is Poe?

Poe is a conversational artificial intelligence platform developed by Quora, the renowned question-and-answer site founded by former Facebook employees. It brings together in a single place the best AI models available on the market, including OpenAI's GPT-4o, Anthropic's Claude 3, Google's Gemini, Meta's Llama, Mistral, and many more. Poe allows users to test, compare, and switch between different AI assistants without having to subscribe to multiple separate and costly plans. The platform also offers a highly popular feature for creating custom bots from user-defined system prompts, enabling users to build their own specialized AI assistants that can be publicly shared. These bots can be monetized by their creators through an integrated revenue system. Poe is available on web, iOS, and Android. It is an ideal solution for developers, AI enthusiasts, researchers, and content creators who wish to explore the entire AI ecosystem without multiplying their expenses, enjoying the best models available worldwide from a single unified application.

FAQ

Does Poe give access to the real GPT-4?
Yes, Poe uses official APIs from OpenAI, Anthropic and Google. You access the real models.
Poe Pro vs separate subscriptions?
Poe Pro at $19.99/month is often cheaper than subscribing to ChatGPT Plus ($20) + Claude Pro ($20) separately.
Can you create bots for free on Poe?
Yes, bot creation and sharing are available on the free plan.
