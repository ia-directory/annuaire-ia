What is Surfer SEO?

Surfer SEO is a professional web content optimization tool based on in-depth and real-time analysis of search engine results page data, commonly known as SERP. Founded in 2017 in Poland by Michał Suski and Tomasz Niezgoda, the platform has become one of the indispensable global references in data-driven search engine optimization. Its operation is based on an automatic comparative analysis of your text in progress against the web pages currently best ranked on Google for a specific target keyword. The real-time content editor assigns a dynamic optimization score that guides the writer step by step, indicating the keywords and semantic expressions to integrate, the optimal word count, the ideal heading structure from H1 to H4, and the important thematic entities to mention. Surfer also offers a comprehensive website audit tool to detect under-optimized pages, a keyword research tool with search intent analysis, and an internal linking tool. Its direct integration with Jasper AI allows simultaneously generating and optimizing entire blog articles. Surfer SEO is the reference tool adopted by SEO agencies, freelance web writers, corporate content teams, and all digital marketing professionals wishing to maximize their organic ranking on Google.
