What is Taskade AI?

Taskade AI is an all-in-one collaborative productivity platform that integrates autonomous artificial intelligence agents directly at the heart of team workflows. Founded in 2019 in San Francisco, the platform combines task management, document creation, mind maps, databases, team chat, and video calls in a single unified space, all enriched by advanced AI capable of automating entire processes. Taskade's AI agents can be configured and customized to execute complex autonomous workflows such as researching information on the web, writing comprehensive reports, managing projects, tracking tasks, or even automatically responding to team members. Its content generation feature allows instantly creating structured task lists, detailed project plans, reference documents, and team wikis from a simple prompt. Taskade also offers ready-to-use AI templates for dozens of professional and personal use cases. Taskade's elegant unified interface, available on web, iOS, Android, and desktop, makes it an ideal tool for startups, communication agencies, product teams, and independent workers seeking to centralize and automate their daily productivity with truly integrated artificial intelligence.
