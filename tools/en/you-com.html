What is You.com?

You.com is an intelligent search engine developed in California that combines traditional web search with advanced conversational capabilities powered by artificial intelligence. Founded in 2020 by Richard Socher, a former Chief Scientist at Salesforce, You.com allows users to obtain direct, synthesized, and sourced answers to their questions while transparently displaying the original source websites consulted. The platform integrates several specialized assistants within a single interface: YouWrite for content writing, YouCode for programming code generation, YouImagine for AI image creation, and YouResearch for in-depth analysis. You.com places particular emphasis on protecting user privacy by limiting advertising tracking. It represents a credible and innovative alternative to Google Search for users who desire a truly AI-augmented search experience, with richer, more reliable, and more comprehensive responses than the simple traditional link lists offered by conventional search engines, making research dramatically faster, more effective, and far more rewarding for anyone seeking accurate and reliable information quickly.

FAQ

Is You.com really private?
Yes, You.com doesn't sell your data to advertisers and offers a private search mode.
You.com vs Perplexity?
Both cite sources. You.com offers more specialized modes. Perplexity is better known and often more accurate.
Can I use You.com for free?
Yes, the free version offers unlimited AI searches with cited sources.
